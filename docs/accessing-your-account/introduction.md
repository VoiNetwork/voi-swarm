# Introduction

Now that you are part of the network it's important to understand how to access your account and manage it.
This section will guide you through the different ways you can access your account and manage it.

Safe guarding your wallet is important at all times,
and it is recommended that you keep all copies of your wallet in cold storage.
If keeping your key online it is recommended
that you ensure that your system is secure and up to date according to best practices.
